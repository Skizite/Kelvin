THE LIST IN HTML

Ther are three list types
There are (1)unordered list (2)ordered list (3)description list

Unordered list is denoted by (ul), and the list items (li). E.g

  • bread
  • milk
  • butter

Ordered list is denoted by (ol)and the list item also (li). E.g

  1. Boil water for 5mins
  2. Mix milk, sugar and bournvita in a mug
  3. Pour the boiled water in the mug and stir

Description list is denoted by (dl). The name of the item is denoted (dt) and the the definition of the item is denoted by (dd). E.g

Bread
An eatable soft foo made of flour
